About the wanderer

Hi, my name is Niki and I like to travel. I find there is nothing more inspiring than travel. It doesn't have to be anywhere fancy or exoctic. Just breaking from your normal surroundings will open your eyes to new places, people, and experiences. This blog is dedicated to the travels I take around this fasticating planet of ours. Will you join me?
